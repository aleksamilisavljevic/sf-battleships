Game Overview

Battleship is a strategic guessing game where two players take turns trying to sink each other's ships. The game is played on a grid, and the goal is to guess the location of the opponent's ships before they find yours.

How to Play

The game takes place on a grid, typically 10x10, for each player. Each player will place their ships on their grid, which can be either horizontal or vertical. After placing the ships, players take turns guessing coordinates to "fire" upon the opponent's grid in order to hit their ships.

Objective

The objective of Battleship is to sink all of the opponent's ships before they sink yours. Each ship has a different size, and each hit or miss is tracked on a separate tracking grid.
